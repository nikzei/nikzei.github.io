Nikki Zeichner

I design digital tools that help city governments provide better social services.

In November, I completed a fellowship with Code for America where I worked as part of a three person team to build open source citizen engagement tools for the City of Vallejo, California. Currently, I research for Code for America's health team, which is focused on improving access to food stamps in California.

I came to Code for America after completing an M.S. in Integrated Digital Media at NYU's Engineering School where I was a Women in Technology fellow and a Project Management Fellow with the GovLab@NYU. Before returning to graduate school, I practiced as a criminal defense attorney in New York and developed multi-media public projects about incarceration in the U.S.
